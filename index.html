TTT WORLDPage 2
Safety
Occupational Health & Safety
⚡🦺[Icon: A hard hat and a warning lightning bolt]
  • 1Safety is your first job. Safety is everyone's job.
  • 2Wear your safety shoes. Wear your gloves. Wear your safety glasses.
  • 3Never touch electric wires. Never touch electric boxes.
  • 4Tell your supervisor if you see a broken wire or a broken plug.
  • 5Never put your hands inside a moving machine.
  • 6Never remove a machine safety cover.
  • 7Stop the machine before you clean it.
  • 8Know where the fire exit is. Know where the fire extinguisher is.
  • 9If you get hurt, tell your supervisor right away.
TTT WORLDPage 3
Hygiene
Hygiene & Clean Room
🧼🥽[Icon: A hand washing with soap, next to a clean room suit]
  • 1Wash your hands with soap before work.
  • 2Wash your hands with soap after the toilet.
  • 3Keep your hair clean. Keep your nails short and clean.
  • 4Keep your uniform clean every day.
  • 5Keep your work table clean and tidy.
  • 6Put trash in the trash bin only.
  • 7The Clean Room has special rules. You must follow all of them.
  • 8Wear the clean room suit, mask, and shoe covers every time.
  • 9No food. No drink. No phone. No jewelry inside the Clean Room.
  • 10Wash your hands before you enter the Clean Room.
TTT WORLDPage 4
During Work
Working Hours Rules
⏰🚬[Icon: A clock with a coffee cup and a "smoking area" sign]
  • 1Come to work on time. Be at your work place on time.
  • 2Wear your ID card at all times.
  • 3Work quietly. Do not shout on the production line.
  • 4Take your break only at break time.
  • 5Eat your meal only in the canteen.
  • 6Smoke only in the smoking area.
  • 7Never smoke on the production line. Never smoke in the Clean Room.
  • 8Ask your supervisor before you leave your work place.
  • 9Turn off your phone or keep it silent during work.
TTT WORLDPage 5
Free Time
Off-Work & Weekend Rules
🏠🍽️[Icon: A house with a clock showing weekend, next to a food tray]
  • 1Be quiet after work. Other workers may be sleeping.
  • 2Respect your roommates at all times.
  • 3Keep the dormitory clean on weekends too.
  • 4Eat your meals at the canteen during open hours.
  • 5Follow the canteen line. Wait for your turn.
  • 6Do not waste food. Take only what you can eat.
  • 7Tell your supervisor before you leave the factory site.
  • 8Come back before the gate closing time.
TTT WORLDPage 6
Living Area
Dormitory & Container Rules
🛏️🚭[Icon: A bunk bed next to a "no smoking" sign]
  • 1Keep your room clean every day.
  • 2Make your bed every morning.
  • 3Keep your things in your own space only.
  • 4Respect your roommates. Keep noise low at night.
  • 5No smoking inside the dormitory.
  • 6No smoking inside the container.
  • 7Smoke only in the outside smoking area.
  • 8Never cook with open fire in your room.
  • 9Report any damage to your supervisor right away.
TTT WORLDPage 7
Production
Quality Control
✅❌[Icon: A magnifying glass over a product, with a green check and a red X]
  • 1Check every product before you pack it.
  • 2Keep your work area in order. Keep parts in the correct box.
  • 3Check the color. Check the shape. Check for scratches.

    Production
    Machine Instructions (Injection)
    🏭🔘[Icon: An injection machine with a big green start button and a red stop button]
    • 1Only trained operators may use the machine.
    • 2Check the machine before you start it.
    • 3Press the start button only when it is safe.
    • 4Watch the machine while it is running.
    • 5Take out the finished part carefully.
    • 6Check every part for defects.
    • 7If you hear a strange sound, press the stop button.
    • 8If there is a problem, call your supervisor right away.
    • 9Never leave a running machine alone.
    TTT WORLDPage 9
    Production
    Assembly Instructions (Syringe)
    💉🖨️[Icon: A syringe part next to a screen-printing frame and assembled syringe]
    • 1Wash your hands before assembly work.
    • 2Check each part before you use it.
    • 3Do not use a broken or dirty part.
    • 4Print the marking carefully and straight.
    • 5Check the print. The print must be clear and correct.
    • 6Put the parts together in the correct order.
    • 7Press the parts together fully. No gaps allowed.
    • 8Check the finished syringe before you place it in the tray.
    • 9Keep your hands clean during the whole process.
    TTT WORLDPage 10
    Production
    Packaging Instructions
    📦📥[Icon: A small inner box being placed inside a bigger outer box]
    • 1Check the product before you pack it.
    • 2Count the correct number of pieces for the inner box.
    • 3Close the inner box carefully. Do not damage the box.
    • 4Put the correct label on the inner box.
    • 5Put the correct number of inner boxes into the outer box.
    • 6Close the outer box tightly with tape.
    • 7Put the correct label on the outer box.
    • 8Stack the boxes carefully. Do not stack too high.
    • 9Report any wrong count to your supervisor.
    TTT WORLDPage 11
    Behavior
    Professional Conduct
    🤝[Icon: Two colleagues, one man and one woman, shaking hands respectfully]
    • 1Respect all colleagues. Respect men and women equally.
    • 2Speak politely to everyone.
    • 3Keep a respectful distance with colleagues.
    • 4No inappropriate touching. No inappropriate words.
    • 5No inappropriate relationships during work hours.
    • 6Keep your behavior professional at all times.
    • 7If someone bothers you, tell your supervisor right away.
    • 8Help each other. Work as one team.
    TTT WORLDPage 12
    Health
    Leave & Health Permissions
    🏥📋[Icon: A hospital cross next to a permission form and a pen]
    • 1Tell your supervisor if you feel sick.
    • 2Ask your supervisor before you go to the clinic or hospital.
    • 3Ask for permission before you take a day off.
    • 4Ask for permission as early as you can.
    • 5Bring a doctor's note after a hospital visit.
    • 6Give the doctor's note to your supervisor or HR.
    • 7Never leave the factory without permission.
    • 8HR is always ready to help you with health matters.
    TTT WORLDPage 13
    Site Rules
    Internet & Boundaries
    📶🚧[Icon: A Wi-Fi signal next to a fence marking the factory boundary]
    • 1Wi-Fi is a benefit for you. Use it with respect.
    • 2Each worker has a Wi-Fi data limit every month.
    • 3Do not share your Wi-Fi password with others.
    • 4Do not download very large files.
    • 5Stay inside the factory boundary at all times.
    • 6Do not cross the factory fence.
    • 7Ask permission before you leave the factory site.
    • 8Follow the site map. Follow the marked walking paths.
